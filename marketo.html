DJ West

International djwest@mheducation.com

DJ West

Vice President of Professional Services, McGraw Hill 

DJ West is a collaborative and results-driven educational sales leader with over a decade of experience at McGraw Hill, guiding high-performing teams and driving impactful learning solutions nationwide. Currently serving as Vice President of Professional Services, DJ specializes in strategic sales planning, sales enablement, and cross-functional collaboration, ensuring sales teams are equipped with the tools, training, and confidence to excel. 

Throughout his career, DJ has led national curriculum and consultant teams, recruited and coached top talent, and designed high-stakes sales presentations for some of the largest districts in the country. His approach emphasizes creating safe, innovative spaces for teams to share ideas, develop new skills, and strengthen client relationships. 

A skilled communicator and public speaker, DJ bridges the gap between sales, product development, marketing, and international divisions, influencing product direction and shaping training initiatives. His background spans K–12 education leadership, higher education instruction in biology, and professional development in science, health, and educational technology. 

DJ holds advanced degrees in biology and theology, as well as graduate studies in school administration, reflecting a career-long commitment to learning and leadership.
